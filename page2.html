WHO

I am Garret. My pronouns are he/his. I mainly do video editing and camera work, but I enjoy learning and making stuff in a wide variety of digital mediums. I've used almost every available software in the adobe suite, I have learned Python, Java, C#, C++, and probably some others as well in a more rudimentary way.

I've been making digital stuff since I was first able to use a computer. Messing around in free video editing software and game engines. Unfortunately I no longer have a lot of the stuff that I made a while ago, but I hope that this site can hold my current and future work!

During my free time I like to consume creative media to gather ideas for future projects. YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, everything has a little something that you could take away from it. I really like all types of stuff though, so if you have anything that fits that let me know! I would be more than happy to have more interesting stuff to look at.

Oh I also really like animals! I've had animals my whole life and they are just the best!
